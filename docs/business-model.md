# Business Model: Community Call Centre Operations

## Classification
- Repository: `cloud-itonami-8220`
- ISIC Rev.5: `8220` — activities of call centres
- Social impact: customer data privacy, agent worker safety, local
  jobs

## Customer
- independent/community call-centre operators needing an auditable
  data-handling and quality-assurance platform
- client businesses outsourcing inbound/outbound customer contact
  needing verifiable compliance and quality records
- regulators needing verifiable data-handling-scope and
  telemarketing-compliance records
- programs that cannot accept closed, unauditable call-centre
  platforms

## Offer
- data-handling and compliance-scope management
- robotics-assisted first-line voice/chat triage and screen-pop
  automation
- agent registration, dispatch and quality-assurance records
- client billing and disclosure records
- role-based access and immutable audit ledger

## Revenue
- self-host setup fee
- managed hosting subscription per seat/campaign
- support retainer with SLA
- voice/chat-triage automation integration and maintenance

## Trust Controls
- a robot action the governor refuses is never dispatched
- safety-critical actions (a data-handling action outside verified
  compliance scope, an agent match without a completed registration/
  training check, an unverified quality record) require human
  sign-off
- agents cannot be dispatched outside verified compliance scope
- quality records require verified evidence
- sensitive customer and call data stays outside Git
